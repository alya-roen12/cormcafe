About
Us

CORM Café, founded in 2014 in Jasin, Melaka by a full-time teacher turned entrepreneur, was born out of passion and purpose. The name “CORM” symbolises underground growth and quiet strength, reflecting the café’s commitment to authenticity and resilience. Originally launched as an online indie product distributor, it has evolved into more than just a cafe. Corm serves as a creative hub and community sanctuary that fosters counter-culture and intellectual exchange. With indie roots and a strong focus on originality, CORM Café began with a small, dedicated team of just three members, offering personalized service while championing a space where creativity and meaningful conversation thrive.
